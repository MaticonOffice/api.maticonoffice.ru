# Get Maticon Office DocSpace

Maticon Office offers these ways to access and use DocSpace:

## Cloud version

This method requires no installation. [Sign up to DocSpace](https://www.maticonoffice.ru/docspace-registration?utm_source=api&utm_medium=article&utm_campaign=docspaceapi) to get started and access your DocSpace portal.

## Local server

[Maticon Office DocSpace](https://www.maticonoffice.ru/download-developer.aspx?from=api#docspace-developer) is available for Windows, Linux, Docker, and Amazon AWS. Follow the instructions in **Maticon Office Help Center** to install on your local server:

- [Get Maticon Office DocSpace for Windows](https://help.maticonoffice.ru/docspace/installation/docspace-developer-install-windows.aspx?from=api)
- [Get Maticon Office DocSpace for Linux](https://help.maticonoffice.ru/docspace/installation/docspace-developer-install-script.aspx?from=api)
- [Get Maticon Office DocSpace for Docker](https://help.maticonoffice.ru/docspace/installation/docspace-developer-install-script.aspx?from=api)
- [Get Maticon Office DocSpace for Amazon AWS](https://help.maticonoffice.ru/docspace/installation/docspace-developer-install-amazon.aspx?from=api)
